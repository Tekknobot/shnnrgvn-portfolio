South Asian Night Kitchen & Bar

Dine-In & Late-Night Pick-Up

Nakatomi Nights weaves classic comfort dishes with the heat, smoke, and perfume of South Asia. Small plates meant for sharing, cocktails laced with chai, tamarind and mango, and a playlist that never sleeps.
