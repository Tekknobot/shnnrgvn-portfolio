Practice

The documents that quietly run your company.

Rather than trying to be “full service”, we focus on a narrow band of work that moves the needle for tech teams in Toronto’s ecosystem.

Startup Formation & Founder Alignment

Incorporation strategy for Ontario and federal corporations, founder agreements, vesting, cap table design, advisory equity and early employee incentive plans.

Financing Rounds & Investor Docs

SAFEs, convertible notes, priced rounds, side letters and shareholder agreements – with negotiation support tailored to Canadian and U.S. investors.

Product, Privacy & Data

Terms of use, privacy policies, DPA/DPAs, data-sharing relationships and internal playbooks aligned with Canadian privacy laws and global norms.

Commercial & Technology Contracts

SaaS and licensing agreements, pilot projects, reseller and partnership deals, NDAs and MSAs that protect your IP without derailing sales cycles.

If your needs fall outside this lane – employment disputes, complex litigation, tax restructuring – we tap a small network of Toronto specialists while remaining your first call and strategic translator.

Pricing is transparent: flat-fee menus for common projects, and subscription counsel for growing teams that need on-call help without in-house overhead.

Typical entry engagements: pre-seed formation packages, review of a lead investor’s term sheet, or a full refresh of your product’s legal surface before launch.

Process

A legal workflow that respects your sprint cadence.

The work is structured for founders who live in Figma, Notion and GitHub – not in boardrooms. No mystery bills, no eight-person conference calls.

01 · Orientation

Thirty minutes of brutal clarity

We map your next funding, product and hiring milestones, review existing docs and pinpoint the top three legal gaps that could actually hurt you.

02 · Build

The smallest stack that works

We draft or upgrade the agreements your team uses weekly – the pieces you can actually operate confidently without a lawyer in every email thread.

03 · Run

On-call as you negotiate

As deals and hires progress, we sit on your side of the table, helping you prioritize what to push on and what to let through to keep momentum without giving up leverage.

Communication runs through email and shared workspaces – with crisp written summaries instead of dense memos. Every deliverable arrives with a one-page “how to use this” guide for your team.

We currently take on a limited number of subscription counsel clients each quarter to keep response times tight. If the roster is full, we can work project-by-project until a slot opens.
