About Odd Finds

Tucked along Bloor Street West in Bloordale, Odd Finds General Store is a neighbourhood spot for the delightfully unexpected: vintage glassware, second-hand books, art prints, candles, textiles, and one-off curiosities.

Every item on the shelf is handpicked – chosen for its story, its patina, or just because it made us smile. We believe in giving objects a second life, supporting local makers, and keeping our corner of the city a little bit weird.

What you’ll find

  • Vintage & second-hand homewares
  • Retro decor & artwork
  • Books, zines & printed matter
  • Local maker goods & gifts
  • Seasonal pop-ups & mini markets
