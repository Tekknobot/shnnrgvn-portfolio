What we do

Painting Services

The Great Painting provides interior and exterior painting services across Toronto and the GTA. Our experienced painting contractors work on homes, condos, heritage properties, and commercial spaces, delivering clean, long-lasting finishes with careful prep and attention to detail.

Light, clean interiors that feel calm and finished, with attention to edges, trim, and existing finishes.

  • Walls, ceilings, trim, and doors
  • Living rooms, bedrooms, hallways, and stairwells
  • Kitchen and bathroom repaints
  • Feature / accent walls and colour blocking
  • Cabinet and built-in repainting (spray or brush & roll)
  • Low-VOC options for sensitive homes

Durable exterior work that respects your home’s character and stands up to Toronto weather.

  • Brick, stucco, siding, and wood cladding
  • Porches, soffits, fascia, and trim details
  • Front doors, garages, and shutters
  • Decks, fences, railings, and metalwork
  • Colour updates for curb appeal & resale
  • Season-appropriate scheduling and products

Long-lasting paint jobs start with prep. We take time to fix surfaces properly before the first coat goes on.

  • Careful masking and protection of floors & furniture
  • Scraping, sanding, and feathering rough areas
  • Filling holes, cracks, and nail pops
  • Minor drywall repair and skim coating
  • Caulking gaps at trim and casings
  • Priming and stain / tannin blocking where needed

Small details that make a home feel finished and thoughtfully put together.

  • Two-tone doors, frames, and baseboards
  • Stairs, handrails, and spindles
  • Window trims and sash touch-ups
  • Board-and-batten and wainscoting painting
  • Ceiling colour adjustments to soften light
  • Subtle sheens matched to how each room is used

We work on homes and small businesses across Toronto and the GTA.

  • Single-family homes and semi-detached houses
  • Condos, townhomes, and rental units
  • Heritage and older character properties
  • Cafés, bakeries, and small retail spaces
  • Offices, studios, and common areas
  • Turnover repaints between tenants or owners

A simple, clear process so you always know what’s happening next.

  • Quick conversation to understand your project
  • On-site visit and written, itemized estimate
  • Help with colour choices and finishes if needed
  • Scheduled start date with clear timelines
  • Daily site protection, ventilation, and clean-up
  • Walk-through at the end and touch-ups before we leave
Not seeing your exact project? We regularly handle one-off requests like stair refinishing, single-room refreshes, and small commercial touch-ups. If paint is involved, we can likely help—just ask in your initial message.
